About Me

Originally from Egypt, born and bred, I moved to the states in 2005. As most of new immigrants do, I settled on the east cost for a couple of years before moving to the to Seattle area in 2007 and have fallen in love with the Pacific Northwest. To date my professional experience has focused on community pharmacy. I recently stumbled upon an opportunity to learn about web development and discovered a new passion. I enrolled as a student with University of Washington's Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp and graduated the program in September 2021..

I believe life is a journey of continuos learning. This is even more so in the rapidly, ever changing field of Technology. As I learn more about becoming a web developer, I'm hoping to bring my drive, passion, and prior knowledge into this new career.

For more information about my professional experience, please refer to my resume.

Outside of work, I am a husband and a father to three beautiful girls who keep us busy with all of their activities. We like to spend time outdoors as a family and we love to travel. I am also a huge soccer fan and I play indoor frequently.
